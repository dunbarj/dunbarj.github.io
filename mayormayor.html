Huh?

Mayor! Mayor! is a mix of a lot of things. If the simulator, idle, and strategy genres had some sort of weird baby, this is it! If you like watching virtual cities earn virtual money then this is the game for you. Plan out cities around the globe to make money and keep people happy!
