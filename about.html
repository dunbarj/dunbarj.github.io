About Me

Hi! My name is Jacob Dunbar and I am a senior at Purdue University studying Computer Science with concentrations in Software Engineering and Computer Graphics and Visualization.

I am a student leader in Purdue's All-American Marching Band, the "Computer Guru" for the honorary band service fraternity Kappa Kappa Psi, and a founding executive member of the Purdue Independent Game Developer's Club.

I have working/course experience in C/C++, Java, Python, ARM/x86 Assembly, HTML, and CSS.

I also have independent experience with C#, JavaScript, Node.js, and SQL/MySQL.

Computer Science concepts that I have a good grasp of include data structures (stacks, queues, linked lists, tree, hash tables, etc), recursion, graphs, pattern matching, digital logic, pipelining, I/O redirection, pipes, memory allocation, threads, semaphores, mutex locks, socket programming, and iterative and concurrent servers.
