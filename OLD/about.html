About Me

Hi! My name is Jacob Dunbar and I am a senior at Purdue University studying Computer Science with concentrations in Software Engineering and Computer Graphics and Visualization.

I am a former student leader in Purdue's All-American Marching Band, "Computer Guru" for the honorary band service fraternity Kappa Kappa Psi, and founding executive member of the Purdue Independent Game Developer's Club.

I have a lot of experience with Node.js and Unity development.

I am proficient with C/C++, C#, Java, Javascript, and Python.

I also have experience with Django, HTML/CSS, SQL/MySQL, and ARM/x86 Assembly.
